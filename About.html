Our Story

Sweet Hour Bakery was born from a passion for baking and a desire to bring warmth to the community. Founded by Ruhma Nazim, the bakery started in a cozy kitchen and grew into a neighborhood favorite — known for creativity, comfort, and deliciousness. Every dessert we bake is a labor of love, made for celebrations, milestones, and those quiet “just because” moments. From custom cakes to everyday cookies, we believe every hour should be a Sweet Hour.
